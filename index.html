🧬 VM Agent-Native

The project to make Varun Malhotra the most AI-native executive in federal consulting. Not AI-curious. Not AI-augmented. AI-native.

Mission

A single human, backed by a world-class agent system, operating with the intelligence surface of a 50-person team.

Real-time market intelligence. Automated opportunity capture. Multi-model orchestration that optimizes itself. Institutional memory that never forgets. All of it composable, auditable, and continuously improving.

📈
